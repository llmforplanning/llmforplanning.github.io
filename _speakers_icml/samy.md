**Bio**: 
Samy Bengio is a senior director of ML research at Apple since 2021, and Adjunct Professor at EPFL, Switzerland, since 2024. Previously, he was a distinguished scientist at Google Research since 2007 where he was heading part of the Google Brain team. Before that, he was at IDIAP, where he co-created the famous open-source Torch library. He is interested in many areas of machine learning research, such as deep architectures, representation learning, vision and language processing, and reasoning.
