**Bio**: 
Noam Brown is a Research Scientist at OpenAI working on multi-step reasoning, self-play, and multi-agent AI. He previously worked at FAIR (Meta), where he helped developing CICERO, the first AI to achieve human-level performance in the strategy game Diplomacy. He was also part of the project that created the first AI to defeat top humans in no-limit poker.
